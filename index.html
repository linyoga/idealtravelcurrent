Our Story

"Early years of wandering are lifetime nutrients."-Lin Hwai-Min, Founder of Cloud Gate Dance Theatre of Taiwan

They sat on a beach, a couple of ideaistic kids, looking out at the skyline and the setting sun. They wondered what lay out there, over the sea and past the horizon. The world seemed so big and yet they were already imagining all the possible places they could go and see once they grew up. Their names are inconsequential—they could be anyone. But what matters is that they met a time in their lives when they still believed.

They quickly became close friends, sharing their dreams and worries about life. Through all the laughter and tears, they came to believe in one another, trust each other, and know that they would always hold this bond no matter where their lives took them.

Years later, most of them ended up scattered across the globe. Some remained in Taiwan, while some went to school or work in the U.S or China or the UK or Europe. But no matter where they ended up, they held on to their promise—the dream they shared when they were kids. This dream was to explore the larger world together and share their adventures with one another. They held on to this promise even as the years passed by.

I have been and always will be one of those kids. This story, !dea travel, is the story of not only how people, but also ideas can travel. It marks the culmination as well as the beginning of our promise and our passion to make our dream come true. It never seems truly started, as I always say, and it will never truly end. I still believe…

"Why so serious?" - The Joker

I am Kory Chen, a young soul who was born in Taiwan on the Christmas Eve some years ago (Don't you ask!). Although I have spent most of my life in this tiny, beautiful island, I always have a big dream of going beyond the horizon and exploring the world. Now until I have been to more than 30 countries, I realize that traveling is just there, in my blood. To me, travel is not just a hobby, but the passion that has shaped who I am today and will drive me further into the future. So far, Turkey and Peru remain my favorites, and Spanish food is the killer.

I am home; and this is the only reason I can go on my next adventure. So, here I am, inviting you, my unknown friend, to share your adventure and join mine as well. I am Kory Chen, welcome to Taiwan, my wonderland.
